Hello, I am Elizabeth Shackelford, a visual artist and aspiring front-end developer, UI/UX designer and visual designer, based in Portland, Oregon. As a visual artist, I specialize in oil-based colored pencil and graphite, and my work primarily centers around pet, animal and human portraiture; female empowerment; inner magic; and imaginative realism. I have two art businesses -- Furever Drawn and Elizabeth Casey Shackelford. Furever Drawn features my custom pet and animal portraiture, and Elizabeth Casey Shackelford features my work regarding female empowerment, inner magic, human portraiture, and imaginative realism.

I received my Bachelor of Arts in Interdisciplinary Studies from Marylhurst University in 2011. The concentration of my B.A. was self-designed, primarily incorporating fine art and psychology, in examining play as the language of the creative core, and its vital role in facilitating and guiding human development and healing. My passion for creativity, visual communication, the arts, interdisciplinary studies, visual design, front-end development, creative problem solving, and the relationship between art and technology led me to not ony becoming a visual artist, but to pursuing my studies in front-end development, visual design, user experience design, and information technology.
